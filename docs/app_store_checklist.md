# App Store Preparation Checklist

- Confirm no personal data collection.
- Confirm no tracking SDKs or advertising identifiers.
- Confirm no account requirement.
- Confirm no internet dependency for core play.
- Replace placeholder audio and app icons before release.
- Review abstract conflict presentation for age rating.
- Test on multiple portrait iPhone sizes.
- Verify local save recovery from corrupt files.
- Verify settings persistence.
- Prepare screenshots showing actual gameplay.
- Prepare privacy nutrition label as no data collected, if the final app remains offline-only.
